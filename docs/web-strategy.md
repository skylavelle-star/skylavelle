# skylavelle.com.au — Web Strategy

## Purpose

skylavelle.com.au is Sky Lavelle's personal authority and professional reputation site.

It exists to establish:
- Credibility as a senior project delivery consultant
- Depth of experience in complex technology and transformation delivery
- Thought leadership in project management, PMO and delivery governance
- Trust that supports consulting and advisory engagements

It is NOT a product store, NOT a template marketplace and NOT a commercial resource hub.

All commercial PMO products, templates, tools and packs are owned and sold via Lavelle Pty Ltd.

---

## Positioning Statement

Sky Lavelle is a senior project delivery consultant specialising in complex technology delivery, project recovery, governance, procurement and digital transformation.

He is the principal consultant behind Lavelle Pty Ltd, which develops PMO resources, delivery frameworks and commercial project management tools.

---

## Primary Goals

1. Build authority and credibility
2. Demonstrate delivery experience and thinking
3. Drive consulting and advisory enquiries
4. Funnel commercial intent to Lavelle Pty Ltd

---

## Non-Goals (Important)

- Do NOT sell templates or PMO products here
- Do NOT host free tools or downloadable PMO resources
- Do NOT present pricing, checkout links or product packs
- Do NOT position this as a product ecosystem
- Do NOT duplicate Lavelle Pty Ltd content

---

## Target Audience

- Senior executives
- Program and project sponsors
- Delivery leaders
- Organisations seeking consulting or recovery support
- People evaluating Sky's credibility before engaging Lavelle Pty Ltd

- Recovery experiences

---

## Consulting Positioning

Sky provides:
- Advisory support
- Senior project leadership
- Project recovery guidance
- Governance and steering committee support
- Delivery assurance

All consulting conversion should lead to Lavelle Pty Ltd contact or engagement pathways.

---

## Pages That Should NOT Exist

Remove or redirect:
- Templates
- Free Tools
- PMO Resources
- Product Packs
- Checkout pages
- Downloads

If they exist historically:
- Convert into "Moved to Lavelle Pty Ltd" pages only
- Or redirect to lavelleptyltd.com.au

---

## Internal Linking Rule

Every commercial intent link must point to:

**lavelleptyltd.com.au**

Not skylavelle.com.au

---
